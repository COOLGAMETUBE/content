Select platform

Now that you have a better feel for prototyping, you'll want to pick the right device for commercialization.

Microsoft works alongside Broadcom, Intel, and Qualcomm to verify support for Windows 10 IoT Core on several of the silicon vendors' System on Chips (SoC). These IoT Core powered SoCs are used in hundreds of different devices that you can use to commercialize your idea with.

For more information on our SoC Partners, please read our documentation on suggested boards and SoCs.
