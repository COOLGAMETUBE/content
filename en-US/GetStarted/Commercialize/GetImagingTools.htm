Get your imaging tools

Here's what you'll need to create OEM images using the Windows 10 IoT Core ADK add-ons. We recommend downloading the latest version of Windows 10, available here.

Devices and storage

  • Technician PC: Your work PC. This PC should have at least 15 GB of free space for installing the software and for modifying IoT Core images.
  • The silicon you'll be using.
  • Monitor or TV with a dedicated HDMI input. We'll use these tools to verify that the image is loaded and that our sample apps are running.
  • HDMI cable
  • Micro SD card. If your technician PC doesn't include a micro SD slot, you may also need an adapter. We recommend the micro SD cards listed here.

Software

Install the following software on your technician PC:

  • Windows Assessment and Deployment Kit
  • Windows Driver Kit (WDK) 10
  • IoT Core .iso package
  • IoT Core ADK Add-Ons

Samples and drivers

  • An app built for IoT Core: Our samples use the IoT Core Default app, though you can use your own.
  • A driver built for IoT Core: Our samples use the Hello Blinky driver, though you can use your own.
