Register your device

To register your device for commercialization, ensure that your device includes support for SMBIOS so that it complies with the SMBIOS Reference Specification.

Here are the fields you will need to set:

  1. Bit 2 in the BIOS Characteristics Extension Byte 2 field
  2. The length of the Type 1 (System Information) table must be at least 1 Bh bytes
  3. (Table 1, offset 04h) System Manufacturer, (Table 1, offset 05h) System Product Name, (Table 1, offset 19h) System SKU, and (Table 1, offset 1Bh) System Family Fields must be filled out in the SMBIOS (at a minimum).

For more details, please read our documentation on OEM license requirements.
